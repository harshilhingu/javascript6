Darshan Raval

Darshan Raval is an Indian singer, composer, song writer and music producer from Ahmedabad, Gujarat born on 18 october 1994. He was born to Rajendra Raval and Rajal Raval.

In 2014, he started his journey with music from India's Raw Star at the age of 19 and later came up with his first single Pehli Mohabbat. Then with the guidance of Himesh Reshammiya, he sang 'Main Woh Chand' and 'Bekhudi' for his film.

In 2016, he released 'Ab Phirse Jab Baarish', 'Tu Dua Hai' and 'Tuu' written and composed himself. In 2017, he released Tera Zikr followed by 'Tera Zikr (Reprise)', 'Ye Baarish', 'Saari Ki Saari', 'Shab Tum Ho', 'Baarish Lete Aana' and 'Baarish Lete Aana (Unplugged)'. 'Tera Zikr' later on gained popularity making it one of his blockbuster hits in Bollywood.'

In 2018, he sang 'Chogada' and 'Kamariya' which later on became two of his blockbuster hits alnog with 'Chogada unplugged'. Later in 2019, he sang the title track of the film Ek Ladki Ko Dekha Toh Aisa Laga and 'Odhani' from the film Made in China. He also sang 'Do Din' making it a dance hit song of the year.

In 2019, he made a musical year by singing 'Hawa Banke', 'Dil Mera Blast', 'Tu Mileya', 'Asal Mein' followed by 'Asal Mein remix', 'Bhula Dunga', 'Saari Ki Saari 2.0'. He also released 'Tere Naal' along with Tulsi Kumar and 'Ek Tarfa' followed by 'Ek Tarfa reprise' amidst the Covid 19 pandemic.

In 2020, he came up with his first ever album Judaiyaan consisting of six songs followed by Teri Aankhon Mein produced by Manan Bhardwaj on the music label Tseries. In 2021, he released 'Rabba Mehar Kari' which gained much love and popularity. Followed by 'Is Qadar' with Tulsi Kumar, 'Jannat Ve' for his beloved blue family, 'Duniya Chhor Doon' and 'Tera Naam' with Tulsi Kumar which reached 200+ million views on YouTube.
